our history

La Cerveza is proud of our entrepreneurial spirit, the force that drives us to innovate. It's that state of mind that helps us see opportunity where others see only risk. It reminds us that “good enough” is not remotely good enough. Instead, we greet every new idea with the words “What if?” instead of “It won't work.”

In 2021, Computer Science Majors Astriel Golondrina and Adriane Dizon, created a liquor company to be sold all over the world. The partners chose the name “La Cerveza” as a Spanish term for "The Beer". They chose the Spanish language as many of their customers around the world are from European countries. Both of the creators of La Cerveza did not expect that their business would grow around the world in just a snap of a finger.

Their purpose is to create a future with more toasts -- a future where everyone can celebrate their wins and even defeats in life. Where we dream big for our customer's moments to be remembered. With just a short of time, La Cerveza witnessed countless new experiences and connections made because of beer. La Cerveza is very committed to producing more high-quality beers around the world for the next century.
